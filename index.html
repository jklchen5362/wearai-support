❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI Virtual Try-On feature work?
Go to the "Try-On" tab, choose one of the built-in models or upload your own photo, select clothing items from your virtual closet, and click the try-on button to see a realistic preview of the outfit.
What features do I get with WearAI Pro?
WearAI Pro unlocks unlimited closet storage (free tier is limited to 100 items), cloud-based photorealistic try-on synthesis (powered by fal.ai & Gemini), automated smart calendar event reminders, and full closet insights.
How do I restore my purchases on a new device?
To restore your WearAI Pro subscription, navigate to the Profile tab, tap "Upgrade to Pro", and click the "Restore Purchase" link at the bottom. Enter your Apple ID details when prompted.

🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

WearAI ("we", "our", or "us") operates the WearAI iOS Application. We are committed to protecting your personal data and privacy.

1. Data Collected & Storage

All clothing details, local categorization tags, and clothing images are saved locally on your device using iOS SwiftData. We do not store or transmit your clothing photos to our servers.

2. Third-Party Cloud AI Services

If you choose to use advanced cloud virtual try-on, selected clothing images and model backgrounds are temporarily sent to secure third-party endpoints (such as Gemini and Fal.ai) strictly for image rendering. These files are processed securely and are not kept or reused by WearAI.

3. System Permissions Required
  • Calendar (EventKit): Local access to show upcoming calendar items for event-based dress recommendation.
  • Camera & Photo Library: To take photos or import images of clothing and user portraits.
  • Location: To access local meteorological data for weather-appropriate styling suggestions.
4. User Rights & Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally, deleting the WearAI app from your iPhone will immediately erase all wardrobe databases, images, and user configurations from your device.

5. Contact Information

For any inquiries regarding this policy, please contact us at support@wearai.jeff.com.
